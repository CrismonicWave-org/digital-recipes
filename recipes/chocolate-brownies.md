# Fudgy Chocolate Brownies

## Description

These ultra-fudgy brownies have a shiny, crackly top and incredibly rich, dense chocolate center. Made with real melted chocolate and cocoa powder for maximum chocolate flavor in every bite.

---

## Ingredients

> **Serving Size:** This recipe makes one 9x13 pan (16 brownies). Scale ingredients accordingly.

### Brownies

- 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
- 8 oz dark chocolate chips or chopped chocolate (60-70% cacao)
- 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
- 4 large eggs
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup chocolate chips (optional, for extra chocolate)

---

## Equipment

- 9x13 inch baking pan
- Parchment paper
- Large microwave-safe bowl or double boiler
- Whisk
- Rubber spatula
- Toothpick for testing

---

## Instructions

### Prep Work (15 minutes)

1. **Preheat oven:** Preheat to 350°F. Line 9x13 pan with parchment paper, leaving overhang on sides for easy removal.

2. **Melt chocolate:** In microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and chopped chocolate. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until smooth (about 2 minutes total). Or use double boiler method.

3. **Cool slightly:** Let chocolate mixture cool for 5 minutes.

### Mix Batter (5 minutes)

4. **Add sugar:** Whisk sugar into cooled chocolate mixture until well combined.

5. **Add eggs:** Add eggs one at a time, whisking well after each addition. Mixture should be smooth and glossy.

6. **Add vanilla:** Stir in vanilla extract.

7. **Add dry ingredients:** Sift together flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gently fold into chocolate mixture with rubber spatula until just combined. Don't overmix!

8. **Add chocolate chips:** If using, fold in chocolate chips.

### Bake (25-30 minutes)

9. **Pour batter:** Pour batter into prepared pan. Spread evenly with spatula.

10. **Bake:** Bake for 25-30 minutes. Brownies are done when toothpick inserted in center comes out with a few moist crumbs (not wet batter). Don't overbake—slightly underdone is better than overdone!

11. **Cool completely:** Let cool in pan for at least 1 hour before cutting. This allows them to set properly.

### Serve

12. **Cut brownies:** Lift brownies out using parchment overhang. Cut into 16 squares with a sharp knife (clean knife between cuts for neat squares).

---

## Chef's Notes

### Pro Tips

- **Don't overbake:** Fudgy brownies should be slightly underdone. They'll continue cooking as they cool.
- **Quality chocolate matters:** Use good chocolate—the flavor comes through!
- **Let them cool:** Warm brownies are too soft to cut cleanly. Patience is key.
- **Clean cuts:** Wipe knife between cuts, or use plastic knife for cleanest edges.

### Variations

- **Nuts:** Add 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
- **Swirl:** Swirl in 1/2 cup peanut butter or cream cheese before baking
- **Mint:** Add 1 tsp peppermint extract
- **Espresso:** Add 1 tbsp instant espresso powder to enhance chocolate flavor

### Substitutions

- **Gluten-free:** Use 1:1 gluten-free flour blend
- **Dairy-free:** Use dairy-free chocolate and vegan butter
- **Less sweet:** Reduce sugar to 1 1/2 cups

### Storage

- **Room temperature:** Store in airtight container for 4-5 days
- **Refrigerate:** For longer storage, refrigerate up to 1 week
- **Freeze:** Wrap individually and freeze up to 3 months
