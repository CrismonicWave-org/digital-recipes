# Gluent Free Chicken Parmesian

## Ingrediants

> These ingrediant amounts are for one serving which is one checken breast.  Multiply to achieve four servings.

- Shredded Parmisian Cheese
  - 1 cup
- Chicken Breasts (Thinner is better, might have to butterfly them)
  - 1 per person
- Marinara (Try for Organic)
  - 1 cup
- Minced Garlic
  - 1 Tablesppon heaping
- Nutmeg
  - 1 Tablespoom heaping
- Blanched Almonds
  - 1 cup to be minced (We use a bullet grinder but other kitchen devices will work)
- Whole Wipping Cream
  - 1 Cup
- Butter
  - 1 Tablespoon, prefer soft aka room temp
- Gluten Free Pasta
  - 2 cups
- Egg, whipped
  - 1 per two servings

## Instructions

This meal is usually cooked in an air fryer but you can bake it too.

- Defrost and possibly butterfly Chicken Breasts
- Mince up the blanched almonds to almost a power, this becomes the base fo the Parmisan crust
- Mince up the shreaded parmisian cheese, the almonds and the minced up cheese mix together with Agg to become the final crust for the chicken
