# Crispy Mozzarella Sticks

## Description

These homemade mozzarella sticks feature a perfectly crispy golden coating with stretchy, melted mozzarella inside. Baked or air-fried for less mess, and served with warm marinara for dipping.

---

## Ingredients

> **Serving Size:** This recipe makes 12-16 mozzarella sticks (4 servings). Scale ingredients accordingly.

### Mozzarella Sticks

- 1 lb block mozzarella cheese (not fresh mozzarella)
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tbsp milk
- 1 1/2 cups Italian breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Cooking spray

### For Serving

- 1 1/2 cups marinara sauce, warmed
- Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

---

## Equipment

- Cutting board and knife
- 3 shallow bowls for breading station
- Baking sheet
- Parchment paper or silicone mat
- Freezer space

---

## Instructions

### Prep Work (15 minutes + freezing time)

1. **Cut cheese:** Cut mozzarella block into sticks about 3-4 inches long and 1/2 inch thick. You should get 12-16 sticks.

2. **Set up breading station:**
   - Bowl 1: Add flour
   - Bowl 2: Whisk together eggs and milk
   - Bowl 3: Mix breadcrumbs, parmesan, Italian seasoning, and garlic powder

3. **Bread mozzarella:** Working with one stick at a time:
   - Coat in flour, shake off excess
   - Dip in egg mixture
   - Roll in breadcrumb mixture, pressing to adhere
   - Place on parchment-lined baking sheet

4. **Double coat (optional but recommended):** For extra-crispy coating, repeat egg and breadcrumb steps.

5. **Freeze:** Place breaded sticks in freezer for at least 2 hours (or up to 3 months). This prevents cheese from leaking during cooking.

### Cooking (15 minutes)

**Oven Method:**

6. **Preheat:** Preheat oven to 425°F. Line baking sheet with parchment.

7. **Arrange:** Place frozen mozzarella sticks on baking sheet, not touching. Spray generously with cooking spray.

8. **Bake:** Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown and crispy. Watch carefully—once cheese starts leaking, they're overdone.

**Air Fryer Method:**

9. **Preheat:** Preheat air fryer to 390°F.

10. **Cook:** Place frozen sticks in single layer. Spray with cooking spray. Air fry for 6-8 minutes until golden.

### Serve

11. **Rest briefly:** Let cool for 2-3 minutes (cheese is lava-hot inside!).

12. **Serve:** Serve with warm marinara sauce for dipping. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.

---

## Chef's Notes

### Pro Tips

- **Freeze is essential:** Don't skip freezing—it prevents cheese blowouts during cooking.
- **Block mozzarella only:** String cheese or fresh mozzarella won't work well. Use low-moisture block mozzarella.
- **Watch closely:** Once cheese starts oozing, they're overdone. Remove immediately.
- **Make ahead:** Keep breaded sticks frozen until ready to cook. Great for parties!

### Substitutions

- **Gluten-free:** Use gluten-free flour and breadcrumbs
- **Different cheese:** Try cheddar, pepper jack, or a mix
- **Panko option:** Use panko breadcrumbs for extra crunch

### Dipping Sauces

- **Classic:** Marinara sauce
- **Ranch:** Ranch dressing
- **Spicy:** Sriracha mayo
- **Garlic butter:** Melted butter with garlic

### Storage

- **Freeze uncooked:** Breaded, frozen sticks keep for 3 months. Cook from frozen.
- **Refrigerate cooked:** Store for 2-3 days. Reheat in oven or air fryer to re-crisp.
