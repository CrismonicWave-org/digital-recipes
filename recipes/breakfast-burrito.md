# Breakfast Burrito with Scrambled Eggs

## Description

Start your day with this protein-packed breakfast burrito featuring fluffy scrambled eggs, melted cheese, and savory toppings all wrapped in a warm flour tortilla. Quick, customizable, and absolutely delicious.

---

## Ingredients

> **Serving Size:** This recipe makes 2 large burritos. Scale ingredients accordingly.

### Main Ingredients

- 4 large eggs
- 2 large (10-inch) flour tortillas
- 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Optional Add-ins

- 1/2 cup cooked breakfast sausage or bacon
- 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
- 1/4 cup diced onions
- 2 tbsp salsa
- 2 tbsp sour cream
- 1/4 avocado, sliced
- Hot sauce to taste

---

## Equipment

- Medium nonstick skillet
- Whisk or fork
- Spatula
- Microwave or skillet for warming tortillas

---

## Instructions

### Prep Work (5 minutes)

1. **Prepare ingredients:** Dice any vegetables. Shred cheese if needed. Crack eggs into a bowl.

2. **Whisk eggs:** Add a pinch of salt and pepper. Whisk until well combined and slightly frothy.

### Cooking (10 minutes)

3. **Heat pan:** Melt 1 tbsp butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.

4. **Cook add-ins:** If using vegetables, sauté bell peppers and onions for 2-3 minutes until softened. Remove and set aside.

5. **Scramble eggs:** Add remaining butter to pan. Pour in whisked eggs. Let sit for 20 seconds, then gently stir with spatula, pushing eggs from edges to center. Cook until just set but still creamy (2-3 minutes). Remove from heat.

6. **Warm tortillas:** Microwave tortillas for 15-20 seconds, or warm in a dry skillet for 10 seconds per side.

### Assembly

7. **Build burrito:** Place tortilla on a plate. Add scrambled eggs down the center, leaving 2 inches on each end.

8. **Add toppings:** Top with cheese, cooked vegetables, salsa, sour cream, and any other desired toppings.

9. **Wrap:** Fold the bottom of tortilla up over filling. Fold in both sides, then roll tightly from bottom to top.

10. **Optional crisp:** Heat seam-side down in skillet for 1 minute to seal and crisp the outside.

---

## Chef's Notes

### Pro Tips

- **Low and slow eggs:** Medium-low heat makes creamier scrambled eggs.
- **Warm tortillas:** Essential for pliable wrapping without tearing.
- **Meal prep:** Make multiple, wrap in foil, freeze up to 1 month. Reheat in microwave for 1-2 minutes.

### Substitutions

- **Gluten-free:** Use corn tortillas or gluten-free wraps.
- **Dairy-free:** Skip cheese or use dairy-free alternative.
- **Protein options:** Add cooked sausage, bacon, ham, or black beans.

### Storage

- **Refrigerate:** Wrapped in foil, 2-3 days.
- **Freeze:** Wrap tightly in foil and plastic wrap. Freeze up to 1 month.
- **Reheat:** Microwave 1-2 minutes until heated through.
