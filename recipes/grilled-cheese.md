# Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

## Description

Golden, crispy bread filled with perfectly melted cheese. This classic grilled cheese sandwich is comfort food at its finest—simple ingredients, flawless technique, and absolutely delicious results.

---

## Ingredients

> **Serving Size:** This recipe makes 2 sandwiches. Scale ingredients accordingly.

### Main Ingredients

- 4 slices bread (white, sourdough, or whole wheat)
- 4 slices American cheese or 4 oz cheddar, sliced
- 3 tbsp butter, softened

### Optional Add-ins

- 2 slices tomato per sandwich
- 4 slices cooked bacon
- Thinly sliced ham or turkey

---

## Equipment

- Skillet or griddle
- Spatula
- Butter knife

---

## Instructions

### Prep Work (5 minutes)

1. **Butter bread:** Spread softened butter generously on one side of each bread slice. Butter should cover edge to edge for even browning.

2. **Assemble:** Place 2 slices bread butter-side down. Top each with 2 slices cheese. Add optional ingredients if desired. Top with remaining bread slices, butter-side up.

### Cooking (5 minutes)

3. **Heat pan:** Preheat skillet or griddle to medium-low heat (300-325°F). Don't rush with high heat!

4. **Cook first side:** Place sandwiches in pan. Cook for 2-3 minutes without moving. Check bottom—should be golden brown.

5. **Flip:** Carefully flip sandwiches with spatula. Cook another 2-3 minutes until second side is golden and cheese is melted.

6. **Press gently:** Use spatula to gently press sandwich down (optional) for better cheese melt.

### Serve

7. **Cut and serve:** Cut diagonally and serve immediately while cheese is hot and melty.

---

## Chef's Notes

### Pro Tips

- **Medium-low heat is key:** Low and slow melts cheese perfectly and prevents burnt bread.
- **Butter to the edges:** Ensures even golden color across entire surface.
- **Cover pan:** Place a lid over sandwiches for 30 seconds to help cheese melt faster.
- **Mayo alternative:** Spread mayo on outside instead of butter for extra-crispy crust.

### Cheese Options

- **Classic:** American cheese (melts smoothly)
- **Sharp flavor:** Sharp cheddar
- **Gourmet:** Gruyère, fontina, or brie
- **Combination:** Mix 2-3 cheese varieties

### Substitutions

- **Gluten-free:** Use gluten-free bread.
- **Dairy-free:** Use dairy-free cheese alternative.
- **Lower fat:** Use cooking spray instead of butter, and reduced-fat cheese.

### Serving Suggestions

- **Tomato soup:** Classic pairing
- **Pickles:** Dill pickles on the side
- **Salad:** Fresh green salad for balance
