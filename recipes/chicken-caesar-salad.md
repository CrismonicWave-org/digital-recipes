# Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad

## Description

This hearty salad features juicy grilled chicken breast over crisp romaine lettuce, tossed with creamy Caesar dressing, parmesan cheese, and crunchy croutons. A satisfying lunch that's both healthy and flavorful.

---

## Ingredients

> **Serving Size:** This recipe makes 4 large salads. Scale ingredients accordingly.

### Chicken

- 2 large chicken breasts (about 1.5 lbs)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Salad

- 2 large heads romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
- 2 cups croutons (store-bought or homemade)
- 3/4 cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
- Lemon wedges for serving

---

## Equipment

- Grill or grill pan
- Tongs
- Meat thermometer
- Large salad bowl
- Sharp knife

---

## Instructions

### Prep Work (15 minutes)

1. **Prep chicken:** Pat chicken breasts dry. If thick, butterfly or pound to even 1/2-inch thickness.

2. **Season chicken:** Rub chicken with olive oil, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper on both sides.

3. **Prep lettuce:** Wash and chop romaine into bite-sized pieces. Spin or pat dry thoroughly.

### Cooking (10 minutes)

4. **Heat grill:** Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat (400°F).

5. **Grill chicken:** Place chicken on grill. Cook for 5-6 minutes per side until internal temperature reaches 165°F and chicken has nice grill marks.

6. **Rest:** Remove chicken from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

### Assembly

7. **Toss salad:** In a large bowl, add romaine and 1/2 cup Caesar dressing. Toss to coat evenly.

8. **Plate:** Divide dressed romaine among 4 plates or bowls.

9. **Slice chicken:** Cut chicken into thin slices on the diagonal.

10. **Top salads:** Top each salad with sliced chicken, croutons, and shredded parmesan. Drizzle with additional dressing if desired. Serve with lemon wedges.

---

## Chef's Notes

### Pro Tips

- **Don't overcook chicken:** Use a meat thermometer. 165°F is perfect.
- **Dry lettuce is key:** Wet lettuce won't hold dressing well.
- **Make ahead:** Grill chicken in advance, store in fridge up to 3 days.

### Substitutions

- **No grill:** Use a grill pan or regular skillet over medium-high heat.
- **Protein options:** Substitute grilled shrimp, salmon, or tofu.
- **Gluten-free:** Use gluten-free croutons.
- **Dairy-free:** Use dairy-free Caesar dressing and skip parmesan.

### Storage

- **Store components separately:** Cooked chicken (3 days), washed lettuce (2 days), dressing (per bottle instructions).
- **Don't dress ahead:** Toss salad with dressing just before serving.
