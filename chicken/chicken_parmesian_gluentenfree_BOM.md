# Gluten-Free Chicken Parmesian - Bill of Materials (4 Servings)

## Shopping List

- [ ] Chicken Breasts (4) - Thinner is better, might have to butterfly them
- [ ] Shredded Parmesian Cheese (4 cups)
- [ ] Blanched Almonds (4 cups) - To be minced into powder (use bullet grinder or food processor)
- [ ] Marinara Sauce (4 cups) - Try for organic
- [ ] Whole Whipping Cream (4 cups)
- [ ] Minced Garlic (4 Tablespoons / ¼ cup heaping)
- [ ] Nutmeg (4 Tablespoons / ¼ cup heaping)
- [ ] Butter (4 Tablespoons / ½ cup) - Prefer soft/room temperature
- [ ] Gluten-Free Pasta (8 cups / approximately 2 lbs dry pasta)
- [ ] Eggs, whole (2) - For whipping/breading

## Equipment Notes

- Air fryer (or conventional oven for baking)
- Bullet grinder or food processor (for mincing almonds)
- Mixing bowl
- Shallow dish (for coating)
