# Blackstone Ribeye & Asparagus Strategy

**Target Sit-Down Time:** 6:15 PM

## 🛒 Prep & Ingredients

### The "Big Four" Dry Rub (1.5x Batch)

*Mix in a small bowl:*

* 3 Tbsp Coarse Kosher Salt
* 1.5 Tbsp Coarse Black Pepper (16-mesh preferred)
* 1.5 Tbsp Garlic Powder
* 1.5 tsp Onion Powder
* 1.5 tsp Smoked Paprika
* 3/4 tsp Dried Thyme

### Finishing Garlic Butter

*Mash together with a fork:*

* 4 Tbsp Unsalted Butter (softened)
* 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
* 1 tsp Fresh Parsley (chopped)
* Pinch of Salt & squeeze of Lemon

### The Asparagus

* Trim woody ends.
* Toss in high-heat oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

---

## 🕒 The 6:15 PM Countdown

| Time | Action | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **5:15 PM** | **Season Steaks** | Use **Worcestershire sauce** as a binder, then apply rub. |
| **5:45 PM** | **Preheat** | Set Blackstone to **Medium-High**. |
| **5:50 PM** | **Well-Done Steak** | Place at 12 o'clock position. |
| **5:55 PM** | **Medium Steak** | Place at 3 o'clock. Start **Asparagus** in a separate zone. |
| **6:00 PM** | **Med-Rare Steaks** | Place at 6 o'clock. Flip other steaks/toss asparagus. |
| **6:07 PM** | **The Pull** | Remove everything to a warm platter. |
| **6:08 PM** | **The Butter** | Dollop **Garlic Butter** on steaks; tent loosely with foil. |
| **6:15 PM** | **Dinner Served** | Slice steaks and serve with charred asparagus. |

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

* **Zone Management:** Use the hotter side for the initial sear, then move "Well Done" steaks to a cooler zone to finish without burning.
* **The "Flavor Toss":** Roll the asparagus through the rendered ribeye fat in the final minute of cooking.
* **The Rest:** Do not skip the 7-minute rest! It allows the butter to meld with the juices for a steakhouse-quality finish.
